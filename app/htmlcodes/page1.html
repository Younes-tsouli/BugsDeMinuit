I don't know, but something just doesn't feel right today. Lately, I have started spending a lot of time alone. I don't know how I'm supposed to fix this. Maybe it's the feeling of being alone, or my love for the old Elm. I still remember the first day we went there. I'm glad Ken showed me this place. The smell of nature, the lake water, the cold air… It was calming. I remember the time I was sitting under the shadow. I felt like… that was my spot. Some people say that we’re too old to go there at night. I can't blame them though. Enough with the talk of fear. I haven't dreamed anything weird today. I think I'm safe for the moment… Until I saw that thing again.
